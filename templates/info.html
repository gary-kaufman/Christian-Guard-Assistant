How To Use This App

Join a room and add at least 10 players with the Add button. Next you can use the Add Roles button to create a secret role for each player. Now each player can click on their name to reveal their role, then click again to hide. The Winning Number is provided at the top! If you add new players, be sure to Create Roles again!

How To Play Christian Guard

The two teams in Christian Guard are Christians and Guards. The Guards have a double agent on their team, the Christian Guard.

The Guards objective is to have the Winning Number of Christians in Jail. The Jail is a designated space where Guards cannot enter, and Christians cannot escape. A Guard can tag up to two Christians and take them to the Jail.

The Christians objective is to have the Winning Number of Christians in the Church. While sneaking around the Guards, the Christians need to share information about who the Christian Guard is and where the Church is.

The Christian Guard (hereafter referred to as CG) should try not to be discovered by the Guards. The CG has the same abilities of Guards. The CG decides the Church location and shares this information with Christians. When at the Jail, the CG can secretly say "Jail Break"; at this point, all Christians must leave the Jail.

The Guards can attempt to Execute the CG. All Guards congregate and if a majority vote is reached, the selected Guard is executed. The game immediately ends, if the Guards selected the CG, they win. Otherwise, the Christians win.

No one can impersonate another role.

No Christian who hasn't been captured, can be in the Jail.
